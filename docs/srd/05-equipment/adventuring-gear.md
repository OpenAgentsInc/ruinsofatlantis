# Adventuring Gear

This section contains item descriptions and tables (e.g., ammunition, packs, and common gear). Many items have Utilize actions with DCs. See the SRD for full item and pricing tables; examples include Acid (25 GP), Alchemist’s Fire (50 GP), Backpack, Ball Bearings, Component Pouch, Healer’s Kit, Holy Water, Oil, Portable Ram, Rations, Rope, Tinderbox, Torch, Waterskin, and more. Specific items like Net, Hunting Trap, Magnifying Glass, and Manacles include special rules and DCs.
