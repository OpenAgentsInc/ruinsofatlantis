# Spiritual Weapon
Level 2 Evocation (Cleric)

- Casting Time: Bonus Action
- Range: 60 feet
- Components: V, S
- Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

Create a floating, spectral weapon that you can move and strike with. When the spell appears, make a melee spell attack against a creature within 5 feet of it; on a hit, it deals 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier Force damage.
