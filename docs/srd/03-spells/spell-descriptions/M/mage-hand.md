# Mage Hand
Conjuration Cantrip (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)

- Casting Time: Action
- Range: 30 feet
- Components: V, S
- Duration: 1 minute

A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range. The hand lasts for the duration or until you cast this spell again. You can use the hand to manipulate an object, open an unlocked door or container, stow or retrieve an item from an open container, or pour the contents out of a vial.
