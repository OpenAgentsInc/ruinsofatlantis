# J Spells

## Jump
Level 1 Transmutation (Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard)

- Casting Time: Action
- Range: Touch
- Components: V, S, M (a grasshopper’s hind leg)
- Duration: 1 minute

You touch a willing creature. The target’s jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.
