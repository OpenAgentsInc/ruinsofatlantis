# Hallow
Level 5 Abjuration (Cleric)

- Casting Time: 24 hours
- Range: Touch
- Components: V, S, M (herbs, oils, and incense worth 1,000+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- Duration: Until dispelled

You touch a point and infuse an area with holy (or unholy) power. The area can be a Sphere with a radius up to 60 feet. The spell affects the area until it is dispelled. When you cast the spell, you can set additional effects, such as creatures of a specified type having Disadvantage on Charisma checks while in the area or preventing such creatures from entering the area.
