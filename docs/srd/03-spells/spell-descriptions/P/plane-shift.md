# Plane Shift
Level 7 Conjuration (Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)

- Casting Time: Action
- Range: Touch
- Components: V, S, M (a forked, metal rod worth 250+ GP and attuned to a plane)
- Duration: Instantaneous

You and up to eight willing creatures who link hands in a circle are transported to a different plane of existence.
